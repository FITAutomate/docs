## Purpose and scope boundaries

Define how FIT manages:

- service plans and partner tiers (entitlements)
- what’s included/excluded by tier
- billing triggers and invoicing artifacts
- access enablement/disablement tied to entitlements
- changes, upgrades/downgrades, and renewals

This SOP is intentionally **not public** (no pricing via chat).

Out of scope:

- publishing exact pricing or internal rate cards publicly
- legal contract drafting (handled by agreements)

## Trigger events

- New client/partner signed
- Plan/tier upgrade or downgrade
- New solution pack added to an account
- Renewal cycle starts
- Non-payment / account pause scenario (if applicable)
- Scope change request that alters entitlements

## Inputs → Outputs (required artifacts)

**Inputs**

- Signed agreement / scope summary
- Plan/tier selection (services + packs + support level)
- Entitlements matrix (canonical)
- Usage/capacity notes (if relevant)
- Billing schedule and terms (internal)

**Outputs**

- Entitlements assignment record (what they are entitled to)
- Invoice + billing record
- Access enablement/disablement record (high-level, references SOP 01/11)
- Change log for plan/tier changes
- Renewal/upgrade notes (feeds SOP 19)

**Templates/logs to standardize**

- `TEMPLATE — Entitlements Matrix (Canonical)`
- `TEMPLATE — Plan/Tier Assignment Form`
- `LOG — Billing & Invoices Register`
- `LOG — Entitlements Changes (Upgrade/Downgrade)`
- `TEMPLATE — Renewal Prep Checklist (Finance Ops)`

## Entitlements model (recommended)

Define entitlements across dimensions:

- Support coverage (baseline async; optional premium)
- Included delivery capacity (hours / workflows / packs — defined contractually)
- Release/update access (which packs, which versions)
- Partner rights (co-branded vs white-label; escalation rights)
- Data handling boundaries (public vs client-confidential KB scope)
- Optional add-ons (e.g., premium Sev1 on-call, reporting/QBR cadence)

## RACI

- **R:** Finance Ops (Fin)
- **A:** Founder/Exec (E)
- **C:** Product Owner (P), Partner Manager (PM), Support Lead (S), Delivery Lead (D)
- **I:** Documentation Lead (Doc), Security/Compliance (Sec)

## KPIs / proof of control

- Invoice accuracy rate
- AR aging (days outstanding)
- % accounts with entitlements matrix assigned and current
- Upgrade/downgrade cycle time
- Churn and retention signals (feeds SOP 19)

## Visibility + publishable summary

- **Internal:** full SOP + entitlements + billing artifacts
- **Public:** no pricing via chat; only high-level “how pricing works” article (already drafted)
- **Do not publish:** rates, discounts, margin details, client billing history, internal thresholds
