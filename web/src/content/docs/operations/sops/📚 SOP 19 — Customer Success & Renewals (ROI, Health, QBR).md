## Purpose and scope boundaries

Turn delivery into durable outcomes by standardizing:

- customer health monitoring
- ROI measurement and reporting cadence
- QBR/Monthly review routines (as applicable)
- renewal readiness steps
- expansion identification (new workflows/packs)

Covers:

- SMB clients on services/solution packs
- agency partners (partner success model is similar but tracked per partner account)

Out of scope:

- public promises of specific ROI outcomes
- publishing client-specific performance data publicly

## Trigger events

- Post-go-live stabilization complete (handoff from delivery)
- Monthly check-in cadence (if included)
- QBR cadence (if included)
- Rising support volume or repeated incidents
- Renewal window opens (e.g., 60–90 days before renewal)
- Partner tier change request

## Inputs → Outputs (required artifacts)

**Inputs**

- Customer goals and success criteria (from onboarding)
- Workflow inventory + critical workflows (SOP 03)
- Support history (tickets, incidents)
- Release history (SOP 09)
- Usage/outcome signals (time saved estimates, throughput, error rates)

**Outputs**

- Customer health score snapshot (simple, explainable)
- Monthly/QBR review summary (wins, issues, next priorities)
- ROI narrative (time saved, risk reduced, outcomes achieved)
- Renewal plan (risks, mitigations, expansion opportunities)
- Customer success backlog (prioritized improvements)

**Templates/logs to standardize**

- `TEMPLATE — Customer Health Scorecard (Simple)`
- `TEMPLATE — Monthly Review Summary`
- `TEMPLATE — QBR Summary (1–2 pages)`
- `TEMPLATE — ROI Snapshot (Time Saved + Risk Reduced)`
- `CHECKLIST — Renewal Readiness`

## Health model (recommended, lightweight)

Track a small set of signals:

- Value realization (are automations being used and producing outcomes?)
- Reliability (error rates, Sev2/Sev1 frequency)
- Support burden (ticket volume trend)
- Change load (how often requirements are shifting)
- Relationship health (responsiveness, clarity, stakeholder alignment)

## RACI

- **R:** Support Lead (S)
- **A:** Founder/Exec (E)
- **C:** Finance Ops (Fin), Product Owner (P), Delivery Lead (D), Partner Manager (PM), Documentation Lead (Doc)
- **I:** Security/Compliance (Sec)

## KPIs / proof of control

- Retention rate (renewals)
- Expansion rate (additional packs/workflows)
- Customer health trend over time
- Incident rate trend (Sev1/Sev2)
- “Time-to-value” for new improvements
- QBR completion rate (where included)

## Visibility + publishable summary

- **Internal:** full SOP + customer scorecards + renewal plans
- **Optional public summary:** “how we measure outcomes and keep systems improving” (no numbers, no client specifics)
- **Do not publish:** client performance data, renewal terms, internal scoring thresholds, sensitive account notes
