## Purpose and scope boundaries

Deliver a consistent “Automation Workflow Assessment” experience that produces:

- ranked opportunities
- safety/guardrails recommendations
- effort/ROI guidance
- a practical phased rollout plan

Includes:

- intake + discovery
- process mapping (current → future)
- opportunity scoring and prioritization
- deliverable packaging and handoff

Out of scope:

- full build/implementation (separate delivery project or solution pack deployment)
- publishing client-specific findings publicly

## Trigger events

- Client requests assessment / audit
- Discovery call indicates “assessment first” best fit
- Partner wants a standardized assessment offer for their clients
- Internal productization effort (improve assessment templates)

## Inputs → Outputs (required artifacts)

**Inputs**

- Discovery notes + goals
- Current workflow examples (emails, forms, spreadsheets, CRM steps)
- Tool stack list + constraints
- Risk boundaries (what must never be automated without approval)
- Sample data (sanitized if needed)

**Outputs**

- Assessment report (structured, repeatable format)
- Opportunity backlog (ranked list)
- Guardrails and approval model recommendations
- Phased plan (30/60/90 or equivalent)
- “Next best action” recommendation (solution pack vs custom build)

**Templates/artifacts to standardize**

- `TEMPLATE — Assessment Intake Questionnaire`
- `TEMPLATE — Workflow Mapping Sheet (Current/Future)`
- `TEMPLATE — Opportunity Scoring Matrix`
- `TEMPLATE — Assessment Report`
- `TEMPLATE — Implementation Roadmap (Phased)`

## Assessment scoring (recommended public-safe approach)

Score each candidate workflow on:

- business value (time saved, risk reduced, revenue impact)
- feasibility (integration readiness, data quality)
- risk level (sensitivity, reversibility)
- maintenance burden (likelihood of upstream changes)

## RACI

- **R:** Delivery Lead (D)
- **A:** Founder/Exec (E)
- **C:** Documentation Lead (Doc), Support Lead (S), Security/Compliance (Sec), Product Owner (P)
- **I:** Partner Manager (PM), Finance Ops (Fin)

## KPIs / proof of control

- Assessment cycle time (intake → delivered report)
- Assessment-to-implementation conversion rate
- Client clarity score (simple “confidence to proceed” question)
- % assessments delivered using standard templates
- Post-assessment satisfaction (CSAT / qualitative feedback)
