# FIT Web — Offerings & Tiers

**FIT Web** is FIT's website solution family. It covers the design, build, and automation infrastructure for client-facing websites—with conversion-optimized structure, clean lead capture, and integrated handoffs into CRM and workflows.

This document is INTERNAL. Do not use content from this page in public chat responses or PUBLIC_WEB KB pages. A public-safe companion page exists for external-facing use.

---

## Overview

FIT Web is structured around three tiers: Lite, Pro, and Premium. Each tier builds on the previous and defines scope, included features, support coverage, and change entitlements.

---

## Tiers

### Lite

**Purpose:** A clean, fast website with structured lead capture and basic CRM handoff.

**Includes:**
- Core site pages (homepage, services, contact) built on approved FIT Web stack
- Structured contact form with CRM routing (basic field mapping)
- Email notification on lead submission
- Basic meta/SEO setup (title, description, OG tags)
- Go-live checklist and operator handoff documentation
- 30-day post-launch stabilization support (async)

**Not included:**
- Intake routing logic beyond basic field mapping
- AI chat or assistant integration
- Custom integrations beyond CRM notification
- Ongoing content updates or redesign requests post-launch

**Support coverage:** Async (email/ticket), Sev1 response within 1 business day

---

### Pro

**Purpose:** A full-featured website with intelligent lead routing, automated follow-up workflows, and operational visibility.

**Includes everything in Lite, plus:**
- Intake routing logic: form submissions routed by type/source/intent
- Automated follow-up sequence (confirmation, owner notification, task creation)
- CRM field hygiene: deduplication, tag assignment, pipeline entry
- Basic chat integration (rule-based routing, not AI)
- Monthly health check: form submission rates, routing accuracy, CRM sync status
- Change request allowance: 2 tuning requests per quarter (scope-controlled)

**Not included:**
- AI chat assistant (requires FIT Knows integration or separate scoping)
- Custom reporting dashboards
- Multi-site or subdomain support

**Support coverage:** Async + scheduled sync (monthly), Sev1 same business day

---

### Premium

**Purpose:** A high-conversion website infrastructure with AI-assisted chat, full workflow integration, and governed change management.

**Includes everything in Pro, plus:**
- AI chat integration (FIT Knows-powered or third-party assistant, configured to PUBLIC_WEB content boundary)
- Advanced intake routing: multi-condition logic, priority scoring, escalation paths
- Full CRM integration: lifecycle stages, activity logging, pipeline automation
- Reporting: weekly lead quality summary, routing accuracy report, conversion funnel view
- Change request allowance: 4 tuning requests per quarter + 1 scoped enhancement per quarter
- Quarterly governance review: routing rules, CTA performance, KB alignment check

**Not included:**
- Bespoke custom development beyond defined stack
- Partner white-label configuration (separate partner track required)

**Support coverage:** Async + scheduled sync (bi-weekly), Sev1 same business day, Sev2 within 2 business days
