# Core Automation Services — What We Build

Forward IT Thinking (FIT) builds **reliable, reviewable automations** and supports teams in adopting AI safely. We work like a product team: clear outcomes, guardrails by design, and solutions that stay maintainable.

This page explains the three core services we offer and what engagements typically look like.

---

## Our Three Core Services

### AI Readiness Audit

A fast, structured assessment that maps your workflows, identifies where AI and automation will actually deliver ROI, and produces a prioritized action plan—with guardrails. The right starting point when you want clarity before you commit.

[Learn more](https://forwarditthinking.com/services/ai-readiness-audit)

### Intelligent Automation

Build and deploy reliable workflow automations—connected to the tools you already use, with human-in-the-loop approvals and exception handling where it matters. Covers intake and routing, inbox workflows, document processing, CRM and sales ops, reporting, and cross-tool integrations.

[Learn more](https://forwarditthinking.com/services/intelligent-automation)

### AI Enablement

Help your team adopt AI safely and consistently. Includes training, playbooks, governance frameworks, and the operational scaffolding needed to embed AI into daily work without creating risk.

[Learn more](https://forwarditthinking.com/services/ai-enablement)

## What We Automate (Common Targets)

- **Intake and routing:** web forms, chat, email requests → assigned owner and next steps
- **Inbox workflows:** triage, labeling, prioritization, task creation, draft responses (review-first)
- **Operations:** approvals, handoffs, reminders, exception queues
- **Documents:** generate, file, rename, route, classify, extract key fields
- **CRM and sales ops:** lead capture → CRM hygiene → follow-up tasks → reporting
- **Reporting:** daily and weekly summaries, KPI refresh, operational rollups
- **Onboarding:** client onboarding packs, internal onboarding tasks, standardized setup

---

## How We Keep Automation Safe

FIT's defaults are designed to avoid surprises:

- **Non-destructive by default:** nothing deletes or sends automatically without explicit design
- **Human-in-the-loop:** humans approve sensitive actions and escalation paths
- **Exception handling:** uncertainty routes to humans instead of guessing
- **Maintainability-first:** fewer moving parts, stable integrations, clear ownership
- **Documented handoff:** so you can operate confidently after go-live

## What Deliverables Typically Look Like

- **Workflow definition:** trigger → process → output + exception paths
- **Configured automation:** connected to your tools and roles
- **Testing outcomes:** validated scenarios and adjustments
- **Operator guidance:** what to monitor, what to do if something fails
- **Change path:** how to request changes safely (tuning vs. enhancement vs. scope change)
