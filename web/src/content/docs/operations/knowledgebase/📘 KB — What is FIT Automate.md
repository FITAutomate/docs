# What is FIT Automate?

**FIT Automate** is the automation and AI practice of **Forward IT Thinking (FIT)**. We help small teams and growing agencies turn repetitive work into **reliable, reviewable workflows**—with human-in-the-loop approvals, clear ownership, and audit-friendly visibility built in from day one.

If you've been burned by brittle "set-and-forget" automations, FIT Automate is designed for the opposite: **automation that stays accountable to the humans responsible for outcomes**.

---

## Who It Is For

### SMBs (operators and leadership)

You want automation that reduces busywork, speeds up handoffs, and improves consistency—without losing control or introducing risk.

### Agencies (delivery partner model)

You sell AI and automation to clients, but you don't want to hire a full delivery team. FIT Automate can support delivery as a co-branded partner, with clear entitlement boundaries and repeatable governance.

## What It Is Not

- Autonomous AI that runs without human review
- Generic templates deployed without configuration
- A platform replacement (FIT works with the tools you already have)

---

## How FIT Automate Works (Delivery Flow)

A disciplined, repeatable approach so automation doesn't drift:

1. **Discover** — map the workflow, owners, bottlenecks, and success criteria
2. **Assess and Plan** — define guardrails, approvals, metrics, and scope
3. **Build** — implement workflows with checkpoints
4. **Test and Implement** — validate with real scenarios; train operators; hand off documentation

For most teams, the fastest and safest start is an **AI Readiness Audit** that produces a prioritized roadmap—then implement the top quick wins first.

---

## What Makes FIT Different

**Human-in-the-loop is the default.** Automations pause when risk is high and route decisions to the right humans.

**Guardrails by design.** Approvals, visibility, and accountability are built into the blueprint—not added later.

**Accountable knowledge systems.** For AI assistants, your documentation is treated as a governed source of truth—so answers are consistent and traceable, not AI guesswork.
