# FIT Knows — Public-safe Overview

**FIT Knows** is FIT's AI knowledge assistant solution family—covering the design, build, and governance of source-backed AI assistants. It answers questions using your approved documents, with clear public/internal content boundaries, retrieval accuracy controls, and ongoing governance.

> Full tier details (Lite / Pro / Premium scope, entitlements, and support coverage) are available to clients and partners through your FIT contact.

---

## Who It Is For

Organizations that want an AI assistant that answers questions consistently and accurately—grounded in your actual documents, not AI guesswork. Useful for internal staff who need fast answers from SOPs and policies, or for external-facing chat that stays within safe, approved content boundaries.

---

## What It Includes

FIT Knows covers:

- Single or multi-channel AI assistant deployment (web widget, internal chat, API)
- Source document ingestion: your approved documents are prepared, structured, and indexed for retrieval
- Content boundary enforcement: the assistant answers only from approved, scoped content—out-of-scope queries are handled with a defined fallback or human escalation
- PUBLIC-facing vs internal content separation: customer-facing assistants use only approved public content; internal assistants have separate, broader scope
- Retrieval accuracy monitoring: answer quality checks, fallback rate tracking, and document coverage gap analysis
- Document update workflow: an approved path for adding, modifying, or retiring source documents after launch
- Governance documentation: what the assistant covers, what it doesn't, how to update it, and how to escalate edge cases

Scope, document volume, retrieval configuration, governance depth, and support coverage are defined by tier (Lite, Pro, Premium).

---

## What It Is Not

- A generic chatbot or FAQ widget with hard-coded responses
- An autonomous AI that acts without human visibility
- A system that trains on public internet data or your competitors' content
- A real-time database query tool (answers are grounded in documents, not live system data)
