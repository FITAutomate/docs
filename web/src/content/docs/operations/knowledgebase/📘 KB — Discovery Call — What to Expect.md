# Discovery Call — What to Expect

The **FIT Discovery Call** is the first step in every engagement. It's a structured, human-led conversation designed to understand how your business operates, where time is being wasted, and whether there's a clear opportunity for **safe, practical AI and automation**.

This is a **free, no-obligation call** focused on clarity—not selling.

---

## Who It Is For

Anyone exploring FIT's services who wants to understand whether AI or automation is a practical fit before committing to anything.

---

## What It Includes

### What We Cover

We'll typically talk through:

- Repetitive daily/weekly tasks that feel frustrating or slow
- Tools you use today (email, documents, spreadsheets, CRM, accounting, chat, etc.)
- Where information is scattered or hard to access
- Where errors, delays, or rework happen
- Tasks that depend on "one person who knows how to do it"
- What you want to improve over the next 3–6 months

You don't need to prepare documentation. FIT guides the conversation end-to-end.

### What FIT Is Listening For

We look for signals like:
- Repeated manual steps and copy/paste workflows
- Inbox overload and missed handoffs
- Disconnected systems that don't talk to each other
- Unclear or undocumented processes
- Questions that get asked repeatedly inside the business

### What You Should Bring (Optional but Helpful)

- Your top 1–3 biggest workflow pain points
- A quick list of tools involved
- 2–5 examples of real work (a sample form, a typical email thread, a report, a spreadsheet)
- Any "hard rules" (what should never happen; approvals required; sensitive data considerations)
- Who is involved (the people who approve steps or receive outputs)

You will **never** be asked to share passwords. Access is handled using secure, least-privilege methods when needed.

### Who Should Attend

- The person who owns the workflow outcome (owner, operator, or manager)
- Anyone who does the work day-to-day
- Optionally: a technical contact if integrations are involved

### What You Get by the End of the Call

- A clearer understanding of where time is being lost
- An initial sense of what AI or automation could improve (and what to avoid)
- A recommendation on whether any FIT service is a good fit right now

Possible next steps:
- Move into an **AI Readiness Audit** for full prioritization
- Proceed with **Intelligent Automation** for a specific well-defined workflow
- Explore **AI Enablement** for team readiness and training
- Decide not to move forward (no pressure)

---

## What It Is Not

- A sales pitch
- A technical deep-dive
- An implementation session
- A commitment to purchase services
- A replacement for detailed analysis (that comes later, if needed)
