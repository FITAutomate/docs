# What is FIT Automate?

**FIT Automate** is the automation and knowledge-systems arm of **Forward IT Thinking (FIT)**. We help small teams and growing agencies turn repetitive work into **reliable, reviewable workflows**—with **human-in-the-loop approvals**, **clear ownership**, and **audit-friendly visibility** built in from day one. website-copy

If you’ve been burned by brittle “set-and-forget” automations, FIT Automate is designed for the opposite: **automation that stays accountable to the humans responsible for outcomes**. website-copy

------

## Who FIT Automate is for

### SMBs (operators and leadership)

You want automation that reduces busywork, speeds up handoffs, and improves consistency—without losing control or introducing risk.

### Agencies (partner/enablement model)

You sell AI/automation to clients, but you don’t want to hire a full delivery team. FIT Automate can plug in as a **co-branded delivery partner (“Powered by FIT”)**, with an optional higher-tier **white-label** track for qualified partners. website-copy

> **Important:** “Solution packs” are not generic templates. They’re deployed with your tools, your roles, and your approvals—so humans stay responsible and informed.
