# Intelligent Automation

## Scale Operations Faster With Guardrails Built In

Design automations that amplify your team while keeping approvals front and center. This service connects your systems, reduces repetitive work, and keeps people in control when context matters.

## Who This Is For

- Teams overloaded by repetitive follow-ups, status updates, and routine approvals.
- Operations leaders who must protect compliance, security, and brand voice while scaling.
- SMB teams that want explicit handoff points, not black-box autonomy.

## What You Get

- Secure workflows that orchestrate CRM, finance, and workflow tools while flagging sensitive decisions.
- Embedded checkpoints that trigger human review on transactions, customer replies, and governance events.
- Monitoring and alerts so you always know what is running, waiting, or blocked.

## How It Works

1. **Define the flow**: Map systems, workflow logic, and approval checkpoints.
2. **Prototype safely**: Build controlled automation prototypes around real operator responsibilities.
3. **Layer guardrails**: Add data, consent, and escalation controls with phased testing.
4. **Train and monitor**: Enable teams with practical runbooks and live visibility dashboards.

## Real-World Examples

### CRM Update Reliability Flow

- **Situation**: Manual record updates miss context and expose compliance risk.
- **Automation**: Template-driven updates with review prompts before status changes.
- **Guardrails**: Human override required for sensitive field edits.
- **Outcome**: Cleaner CRM data and clear accountability for every high-impact update.

### Proposal Intake and Redline Routing

- **Situation**: Sales, finance, and legal approvals are inconsistent and slow.
- **Automation**: Automated routing with reviewer-specific checklists.
- **Guardrails**: Routing pauses until each required reviewer approves.
- **Outcome**: Faster sign-off with a complete, auditable approval trail.

## FAQ

### How do we stay in the loop after launch?

Dashboards and scheduled reviews show what ran, skipped, failed, or is waiting for approval.

### Do you use custom code or no-code tools?

Both. We combine no-code orchestration with custom connectors based on your stack and security requirements.

### Can we pause automation quickly?

Yes. Every build includes manual pause controls and review queues.

### What if data or context changes mid-flow?

We design error handling and reroute paths so humans can intervene before customer impact.
