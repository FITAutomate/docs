# AI Readiness Audit

## Find the Highest-Value AI Opportunities Before You Build

Pinpoint the automation pathways that create measurable business impact without risking compliance, approvals, or data control. This audit gives your team a practical roadmap with guardrails ready from day one.

## Who This Is For

- SMB leaders who need a secure runway before automating approvals or customer touchpoints.
- Ops teams responsible for finance, HR, or compliance-heavy workflows.
- Organizations that need a clear human-in-the-loop model before scaling AI support.

## What You Get

- A recorded map of workflows, data sources, and decision owners that can be automated safely.
- A risk heat map highlighting control gaps, sensitive data paths, and compliance touchpoints.
- Alignment notes, approval gates, and rollout priorities for each automation candidate.

## How It Works

1. **Align on goals**: A kickoff session defines outcomes, guardrails, and decision ownership.
2. **Document the work**: Interviews and process mapping capture approvals, data boundaries, and edge cases.
3. **Score opportunities**: Each idea is rated for value, confidence, and control.
4. **Deliver the playbook**: Recommendations are packaged with people, policy, and verification steps.

## Example Scenarios

### Finance Vendor Approval Flow

- **Situation**: Manual routing causes delays and inconsistent sign-off clarity.
- **Automation**: Structured checklist with staged approvals and automated reminders.
- **Guardrails**: Role-based approvals, audit logging, and mandatory human review before payment.
- **Outcome**: Faster cycle times with CFO visibility and control intact.

### HR Remote Onboarding Workflow

- **Situation**: Forms, policy tasks, and training steps get lost at scale.
- **Automation**: Guided onboarding flow that aggregates data and prompts required reviewers.
- **Guardrails**: Explicit human checkpoints for compliance and final sign-off.
- **Outcome**: More consistent onboarding with a complete audit trail.
